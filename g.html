The Brown Bear

About Brown Bears

The brown bear(Ursus arctos) is native to parts of northern Eurasia and North America. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern. There are many subspecies within the brown bear species, including the Atlas bear and te Himalayan brown bear.

Features

Brown bears are not always completely brown. Some can be reddish or yellowish. They have very large, curved claws and huge paws. Male brown bears are often 30% largwe than female brown bears. They can range from 5 feet feom 9 feet from head to toe.
